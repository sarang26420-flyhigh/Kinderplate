FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about delivery, recipes, or subscriptions? We've got answers.

What ages are KinderPlate meals designed for?

+
Our menus are categorized for children aged 3 to 10 years. We offer soft, simple visual recipes for younger children (3-5 years) and slightly more textured, adventurous flavor profiles for older children (6-10 years).

How do the nutritionist boosts and hidden veggies work?

+
We work with pediatric nutritionists to incorporate micro-nutrients into classic child-approved flavors. For example, our delicious macaroni pasta sauce includes a silky blend of carrots and yellow pumpkins, and our organic chicken nuggets feature hidden chia seeds and quinoa coats. Kids taste their favorite dishes, while their bodies get essential vitamins.

Do you have vegetarian and allergen-friendly options?

+
Yes, absolutely! Over 60% of our weekly menu options are vegetarian. During signup, you can filter for common allergens like dairy, gluten, nuts, and eggs. We isolate our preparation surfaces to prevent cross-contamination.

How does the subscription work? Can I pause or skip?

+
Our subscription is fully flexible. We deliver fresh ingredients in insulated, eco-friendly Kraft boxes every week. If you're going on vacation or want to pause, simply login to your portal and hit "Skip Week" before Wednesday 5:00 PM. No lock-in contracts, zero cancellation fees.

How long do the ingredients stay fresh in delivery?

+
We pack our ingredients in double-walled insulated bags with cooling gel packs. Even if you're not home when the courier arrives, your ingredients will stay chilled and fresh for up to 8 hours on your doorstep. Once unpacked, recipes should be cooked within 5 days.
